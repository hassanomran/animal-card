Husky DOG

The Husky originated in northeastern Siberia and was bred by the Chukchi Eskimos of northeastern Asia to pull heavy loads long distances across rough terrain in difficult conditions

  • scientific name: Canis lupus familiari
  • average length:: 50cm-60cm
  • average lifespan: 11-13 years
  • Habitat: a cold, northern climate

The first dogs arrived in the Americas 12,000 years ago; however, people and their dogs did not settle in the Arctic until the Paleo-Eskimo people 4,500 years ago and then the Thule people 1,000 years ago, both originating from Siberia.The Siberian Husky was originally developed by the Chukchi people of the Chukchi Peninsula in eastern Siberia.
